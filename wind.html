An Interactive Wheater Map Provided By Windy


Explaination

In the top right corner of the map you can choose wich variable you want to show on the map.
you can zoom in and out by holding crtl and scrolling while hovering over the map with your mouse pointer.
You can also zoom in and out with the buttons in the top right corner.
You can also set an marker on the map by clicking on a location on the map, you can now see the value for this location.
If you press the play button it wil show you how the variable you chose will change and progress in the comming days,
You can also drag the date marker left and right.
